# Text

Text represents general content, articles, or documentation that doesn't fit into more specialized content types. It's a flexible format for capturing information that doesn't require the structure of other types.

## Text Usage

Text documents are ideal for:

- General information
- Articles
- Documentation
- Notes
- Research findings
- Reference material
- Conceptual explanations

## Text Structure

While text documents have minimal required structure, they still benefit from clear organization:

- Use headings and subheadings
- Include observations for semantic facts
- Create relations to relevant knowledge items
- Use tags for categorization

## Relations

Text commonly relates to:

- other text documents (references, extensions, or related topics)
- tasks (work documented or described)
- activities (processes documented or described)
- guidelines (procedures referenced or explained)
- persons (authors or subjects)
- organizations (authoring groups or subjects)
