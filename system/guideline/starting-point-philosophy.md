## Guidelines

- Start minimal with what you know and expand based on actual needs
- Focus on essential functionality first, expand when justified by real needs and clarity
- Wait for clarity and actual need to drive solutions, standards and improvements

This philosophy applies to:

- Writing guidelines and workflows
- Software development and architecture
- Documentation and specifications
- Process design and implementation
- Any creative or technical work product
